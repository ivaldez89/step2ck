## Slide 2: The Problem

### Medical Student Crisis

**Burnout Epidemic**
- 44% of medical students experience burnout
- 11% report suicidal ideation during training
- Depression rates 15-30% higher than general population

**Fragmented Learning**
- Students juggle 5-8 different study apps
- $2,000-5,000 spent on learning resources per year
- No integration between studying and wellness

**Isolation**
- 67% report feeling isolated during medical school
- Virtual learning has worsened peer connections
- No platform connects academic and social support

> *"We're training the next generation of healers while breaking them."*
